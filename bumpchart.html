HCI&IV Project - COVID-19 Canada

Interactive Bump Chart

This interactive bump chart represents ranking of Canadian provinces from 1 April until 1 December 2020. The x-axis represents time in months, and the y-axis represents ranking of provinces. When hovering the mouse over some province, the chart becomes interactive and the selected province with its rankings is highlighted.

Data source: Government of Canada - National Health Authority. Link to dataset: https://bit.ly/384eLMy
