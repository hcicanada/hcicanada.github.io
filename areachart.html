HCI&IV Project - COVID-19 Canada

Interactive Area Chart

This interactive area chart shows four different variables in Canada over time: number of total cases, cumulative recovered cases, active cases, and cumulative number of deaths. The x-axis shows time in months from February until the end of December 2020. The y-axis shows number of cases in thousands. The chart is interactive and hovering a mouse over chart, the data of each day is shown separately.

Data source: Government of Canada - National Health Authority. Link to dataset: https://bit.ly/384eLMy
